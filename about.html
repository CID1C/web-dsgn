About Us

Game for Good uses the power of video games to foster education, mental health awareness, and community building. Our mission is to leverage the unique capabilities of gaming to make a positive impact on society.

Our Mission

Our vision is to create spaces where gaming is used not just for entertainment, but to empower individuals and communities. We are focused on three main pillars: education, mental health, and community building.
